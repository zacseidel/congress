About

Allegro Microsystems Inc is a designer, developer, fabless manufacturer, and marketer of sensor ICs and application-specific analog power ICs for automotive and industrial markets. Its Sensor IC allows customers to precisely measure motion, speed, position, and current, and Power ICs include high-temperature and high-voltage capable motor drivers, power management, and LED driver ICs. Its products are divided into three categories: SENSE, which includes Current Sensors, Switches, and Latches, and Interface ICs among others; REGULATE, which includes Regulators, ClearPower Modules, and LED Drive SEMICONDUCTORS & RELATED DEVICES · website
