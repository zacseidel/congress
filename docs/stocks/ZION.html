About

Zions Bancorp is a US regional bank with around $90 billion in total assets as of March 31, 2026. Headquartered in Salt Lake City, Utah, Zions' branch network is in the Western and Southwestern United States and includes seven affiliate banks in these markets. The bank has a heavy focus on small and midsize commercial banking. NATIONAL COMMERCIAL BANKS · website
